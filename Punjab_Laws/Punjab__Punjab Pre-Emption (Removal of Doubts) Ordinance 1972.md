## 1903 Punjab Pre-Emption (Removal of Doubts) Ordinance 1972
 
ORDINANCE XI OF 1972
PUNJAB PRE‑EMPTION (REMOVAL OF DOUBTS) ORDINANCE, 1972
An Ordinance to make provision for the removal of doubts in regard to the existence of right of pre‑emotion in respect of the sale of certain properties
[Gazette of Punjab, Extraordinary, 12th April 1972]
No. Legis. 3 (II)/72.‑The following Ordinance by the Governor of the Punjab is hereby published for general information :‑
Preamble. ‑Whereas doubts have arisen in regard to the existence of right of pre‑emotion in respect of the sale of certain properties ;

**Section 1. Short title and commencement.**
(1) This Ordinance may be called the Punjab Pre‑emotion (Removal of Doubts) Ordinance, 1972.
   (2) It shall come into force at once and shall be deemed to have taken effect from the 7th January 1972.

 

**Section 2. Removal of doubts.**
‑Notwithstanding anything contained !n the Punjab Pre‑emotion Act, 1913 or any other law, or Notification No. 6416‑71/ 3316‑LRIII, dated the 23rd November 1971, issued by the Board of Revenue, Punjab, and published in the Punjab Gazette of 7th January 1972 (hereinafter referred to as the notification), or any decree, judgment or order of any Court, or any proceeding pending before any Court, the notification shall not apply to any sale of agricultural land or other immovable property unless‑

(a) such sale is in respect of the agricultural land or other immovable property described in the Schedule to this Ordinance and is in favour of a person displaced from Mianwali District by the construction of Chashma Barrage ; and
(b) it is certified by the Commissioner of Sargodha Division that such person has been so affected by the construction of the said Barrage.

 

**Section 3. Restoration and fresh disposal of suits etc.**
‑All suits, appeals and other proceedings decided after the issuance of :the notification on the basis of any interpretation of the notification inconsistent with the provisions of this Ordinance shall stand restored and be decided afresh.
