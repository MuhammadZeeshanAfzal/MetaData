## 1102 Punjab Animals Slaughter Control (Amendment) Ordinance 1982
 
ORDINANCE VII OF 1982
(AMENDMENT) ORDINANCE, 1982
An Ordinance further to amend the Punjab Animals Slaughter Control Act, 1963
[Gazette of Punjab, Extraordinary, 26th May 1992]
No. Legis. 3 (7)/82.‑---The following Ordinance by the Governor of the Punjab is hereby published for general information:--
Preamble.‑----Whereas it is expedient to amend the Punjab Animals Slaughter Control Act, 1963 (West Pakistan Act III of 1963), in the manner hereinafter appearing;.
Now, therefore, in pursuance of the Proclamation of the fifth day of July 1977 read with the Laws (Constitution in Force) Order, 1977 (C. M. L. A. Order 1 of 1977) and the Provisional Constitution Order, 1981 (C. M. L. A. Order 1 of 1981), the Governor of the Punjab is pleased to make and promulgate the following Ordinance :‑

**Section 1. Short title and commencement.**
‑--(1) This Ordinance may be called the Punjab Animals Slaughter Control (Amendment) Ordinance, 1982.

 

**Section 2. Amendment of section 2 of West Pakistan Act III of 1963.**
‑In the Punjab Animals Slaughter Control Act, 1963 (West Pakistan Act III of 1963) in section 2,‑
   (a) for clause (e) the following shall be substituted :‑
   "(e) "local authority" means an urban local council constituted under the Punjab Local Government Ordinance, 1979 (VI of 1979) or a Canton ment Board established under the Cantonments Act, 1924 (II of 1924)."
   (b) in clause (k) for the words "Animal Husbandry Department" appear ing in sub‑l clause (v), the words "Livestock and Dairy Development Department" shall be substituted; and
   (c) in clause (1), for the words "Animal Husbandry Department" the words "Livestock and Dairy Development Department" shall be substituted.
