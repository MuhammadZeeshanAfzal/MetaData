## 1672 Punjab Local Government (Amendment) Act 2006
 
ACT VIII OF 2006
Punjab Local Government (Amendment) Act 2006
An Act further to amend the Punjab Local Government Ordinance, 2001
[Gazette of Punjab; Extraordinary, 11th July, 2006]
No.PAP-Legis-2(37)/2006/834, dated 10-7-2006.---The. Local Government (Amendment) Bill 2006, having been passed by the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab on 3rd July, 2006, and assented to by the Governor of the Punjab on 7th July, 2006, is hereby published as an Act of the Provincial Assembly of the Punjab.
Whereas it is expedient further to amend the Punjab Local Government Ordinance, 2001 (XIII of 2001), for the purposes hereinafter appearing;

**Section 1. Short title and commencement.**
--(1) This Act may be called the Punjab Local Government (Amendment) Act; 2006.

 

**Section 2. Amendment of section 42 of the Punjab Ordinance XIII of 2001.**
    ---In the said Ordinance, in section 42, in subsection (4), the words "through a secret ballot" shall be omitted.

 

**Section 3. Amendment of section 69 of the Punjab Ordinance XIII of 2001.**
    ---In the said Ordinance, in section 69, in subsection (4), the words "through a secret ballot" shall be omitted.

 

**Section 4. Repeal**
    --The Punjab Local Government (Amendment) Ordinance, 2006 (IX of 2006) is hereby repealed.
