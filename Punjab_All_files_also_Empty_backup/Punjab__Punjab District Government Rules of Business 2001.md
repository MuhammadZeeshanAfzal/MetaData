## 1301 Punjab District Government Rules of Business 2001
 
PUNJAB DISTRICT GOVERNMENT RULES
OF BUSINESS, 2001
(Amendment of R.16)
10th November, 2010]
No.SOR(LG)5-9/2001, dated 10-11-2010.---In exercise of the powers conferred under section 191 of the Punjab Local Government Ordinance, 2001 (XIII of 2001), the Governor of the Punjab is pleased to direct that the following amendment in the Punjab District Government rules of Business, 2001 shall be made:--
In the said rules, in rule 16, in sub-rule (1), after clause (iii), the following proviso shall be inserted:
"Provided that the Deputy District Officer (Revenue) may make posting and transfer of officials in BS-1 to BS-10."
